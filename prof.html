Hydrology, Micrometeorology, Global Climate Change, and Environmental Biophysics with emphases on
1) turbulent transport of momentum, heat, water vapor, CO2, and other passive and chemical scalars through biosphere-atmosphere interfaces,
2) greenhouse gases emissions from natural and agricultural ecosystems, and
3) ecosystem's photosynthetic response to future climate change.
